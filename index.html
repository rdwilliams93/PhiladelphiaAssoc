Psychotherapy Referral Service At the Philadelphia Association we aim to provide a friendly and informal community service, which enables therapy of high quality to be accessible to all.

Upon contacting us you will be offered an initial meeting with a referrals co-ordinator. At this meeting we will discuss your needs: how much you can afford, your background, the issues you are facing and the kind of therapy you are seeking. If we feel we are able to help you and you would like to proceed, we will then refer you on to the most appropriate therapist for you.

If you would like to arrange an initial meeting please begin by filling in our online questionnaire after which one of our coordinators will contact you.
